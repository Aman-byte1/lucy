Our Mission

Bridging the digital divide across Africa through accessible, high-performance language technology.

Why we built Lucy

Millions of customers are excluded from modern digital services because support systems only speak major global languages. We believe every person deserves to be understood in their native tongue.

Lucy AI was born in Addis Ababa with a single goal: to empower local businesses with the same AI capabilities enjoyed by global giants, but localized for the African context.
